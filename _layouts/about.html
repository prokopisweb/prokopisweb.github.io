Obstetrician-gynecologist

Dr Prokopis Kerimis

Dr. Kerimis received his M.D. from the University of Ioannina, Greece in 1996, going to complete his residency and specialization at the department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at the renowned Haim Sheba Medical Centre - Tel Hashomer, Tel Aviv. During his time at Tel Hashomer he was part of the obstetric team which delivered, on average over 10 thousand babies every year.

During his six years of residency Dr. Kerimis specialized in obstetrics, gynecology, endocrinology with IVF and male fertility. He gained experienced in all types of births; from spontaneous vaginal births to assisted vaginal births (vacuum assisted and forceps assisted), to caesarean births and vaginal births after delivery (VBAC).
